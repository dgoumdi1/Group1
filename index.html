Plan-iT

About Us

What is Plan-iT?

Plan‑iT is a personal productivity companion built to help students and busy people organize their days with clarity and less stress. It brings your plans, groups, and reflections into one simple space so you always know what to focus on next.

Why was Plan-iT built?

Most productivity tools feel either too complicated or too generic for real student life. Plan‑iT started as a practical project to combine software engineering skills with real problems we face every day: balancing classes, work, self‑care, and community.

What can be done with Plan-iT?

With Plan‑iT, you can join accountability groups, track your progress, and manage your daily routines in a way that actually fits your schedule. The website is designed to be lightweight, distraction‑free, and easy to extend as we add new features like habit tracking and smart reminders.

Our Vision

Our long‑term goal is to turn Plan‑iT into a supportive space where people can share goals, celebrate wins, and stay consistent together. This project also grows with us as developers, letting us experiment with new ideas in backend, frontend, and AI while building something genuinely useful.
